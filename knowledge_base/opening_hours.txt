REGULAR OPENING HOURS:
Greenfield Medical Centre is open Monday to Friday 8:00am to 6:30pm. Phones open from 8:00am. Reception open from 8:00am. Last appointment of the day is 4:30pm. Building closes at 6:30pm.

SATURDAY OPENING HOURS:
Greenfield Medical Centre is open Saturday 8:00am to 12:00pm. Saturday morning appointments only. No afternoon appointments on Saturday. All four GPs and Nurse Williams available Saturday mornings. Phone lines open from 8:00am Saturday. Last appointment 11:30am. Building closes at 12:00pm.

SUNDAY AND BANK HOLIDAYS:
Greenfield Medical Centre is closed on Sundays and all bank holidays. No appointments available. For urgent medical advice on Sunday or bank holiday call 111 or visit 111.nhs.uk. For medical emergencies call 999.

OUT OF HOURS URGENT MEDICAL ADVICE:
When surgery is closed for urgent medical advice call NHS 111. Available 24 hours 7 days a week. Call 111 for: urgent medical advice when GP is closed, symptoms that are concerning but not emergency, unsure whether to go to A&E, need same day medical assessment. Do not use 111 for emergencies. Also available online at 111.nhs.uk.

OUT OF HOURS EMERGENCY:
For life threatening emergencies always call 999. Do not call the surgery. Do not call 111. Call 999 for: chest pain, difficulty breathing, stroke symptoms, severe bleeding, loss of consciousness, serious accidents. 999 available 24 hours 7 days a week.

APPOINTMENT TELEPHONE LINES:
Phone lines open at 8:00am Monday to Friday and 8:00am Saturday. Urgent same day appointments released at 8:00am and booked on first come first served basis. Routine appointments booked up to 4 weeks in advance by phone or online. Online booking available via NHS app and Patient Access 24 hours a day.

WALK IN POLICY:
Greenfield Medical Centre does not operate a walk in service. All appointments must be booked in advance. Reception staff can check for cancellations if you attend without appointment but this cannot be guaranteed. For walk in urgent care visit your nearest urgent treatment centre.

REPEAT PRESCRIPTION COLLECTION HOURS:
Prescriptions collected from reception Monday to Friday 8:00am to 6:00pm and Saturday 8:00am to 11:30am. Electronic prescriptions sent directly to your nominated pharmacy automatically.

EARLY MORNING URGENT APPOINTMENTS:
Urgent same day GP appointments are released at 8:00am every weekday morning. These are for patients who need to be seen the same day for urgent but non-emergency conditions. Call at exactly 8:00am to secure an urgent slot. These fill up very quickly. If you cannot get through keep trying or call 111 for advice.

LATE AFTERNOON APPOINTMENTS:
Last GP appointments of the day are at 4:30pm Monday to Friday. Last nurse appointments are at 4:40pm Monday to Friday. If you need an appointment after work these late afternoon slots are most suitable. Book in advance as these are popular.
